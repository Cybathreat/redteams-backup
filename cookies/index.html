Cookie Notice

redteams.ai sets no first-party cookies.

Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, which is cookieless and does not track individuals across sites or devices. No consent banner is required for this processing under the current interpretation of the ePrivacy Directive because no information is stored on or read from your device.

Comments (Giscus)

Article pages include a comments section powered by Giscus, which uses GitHub Discussions. The Giscus widget is lazy-loaded— it does not execute or contact giscus.app / github.com until you actually scroll to the comments area. Once loaded, GitHub may set its own cookies in your browser. Those cookies are governed by GitHub’s privacy statement. If you never scroll to the comments, no connection to GitHub is made.

How to control this

Because we set nothing ourselves, there is no consent to manage on our side. You can block third-party cookies or scripts in your browser; the rest of the Site will continue to function.

For details on all data processing, see the Privacy Policy.
